**Return Fire** was a vehicular combat strategy game produced by Alex Kasper at Silent Software for the 3DO Interactive Multiplayer in 1995 {{< ref "return-fire-review-nextgen" >}}.

## Overview

Return Fire combined action gameplay with strategic elements, allowing players to choose between different military vehicles (jeep, tank, helicopter, armored support vehicle) to capture the enemy's flag while defending their own base.

## Reception

The game was critically acclaimed and became one of the 3DO's most celebrated titles {{< ref "return-fire-egd" >}}. It was later ported to:

- PlayStation
- Windows PC
- Sega Saturn

## Notable Features

- Classical music soundtrack (including Ride of the Valkyries, which became iconic to the game)
- Split-screen multiplayer
- Destructible environments
- Multiple vehicle types with distinct capabilities

## Legacy

Return Fire demonstrated Alex's early career in interactive entertainment before his pivot to cybersecurity work. The game remains a cult classic among 3DO enthusiasts and is frequently cited in discussions of the console's best titles.
