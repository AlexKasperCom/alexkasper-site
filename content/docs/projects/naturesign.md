**Naturesign** is a B2B SaaS platform that automates the generation of regulatory documents for the flavor and fragrance industry.

## The Problem

Companies in the flavor and fragrance industry must produce extensive regulatory documentation for every productâ€”safety data sheets, compliance certificates, ingredient declarations, and more. This documentation is:

- Time-consuming to create manually
- Error-prone when done by hand
- Required by multiple regulatory bodies worldwide
- Constantly needing updates as regulations change

## The Solution

Naturesign automates this entire workflow:

1. **Data Input** - Product formulations and ingredient data are entered once
2. **Document Generation** - The platform automatically produces all required regulatory documents
3. **Compliance Updates** - As regulations change, documents can be regenerated automatically
4. **Multi-format Output** - Documents are produced in formats required by different regulatory bodies

## Technology

Built on Salesforce with custom Lightning Web Components, Naturesign leverages Alex's deep Salesforce expertise to deliver a robust, scalable solution.
